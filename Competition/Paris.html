Bon Voyage to Paris!

Paris - the majestic capital of art, fashion, gastronomy and culture.
Be immersed in the culture as you pass by the Eiffel Tower, Louvre, and Notre Dame and feast on croissant, caviar, and ratatouille.
Be careful of spending way too much euros and always be aware of your surroundings!

Solo female tips

  1. Stay in an all-female room at a hostel!
  2. Have a lock on your bag when you're walking around Paris!
  3. Separate your "small" money (coins) from your "large" money (notes) with two different wallets!

Food and drinks

Paris is of course, a great place for French food.
But looking for something different?
After you check out the Louvre, try heading to the area near the Pyramides station to find authentic Japanese food!

  • Sanukiya - Michelin recommended every year since 2009 and rated 4.5 on tripadvisor. If you're looking for udon noodles, they're the best!
  • Aki Boulanger - check this place out for Japanese cakes and baked goods!
  • Momen'Tea - best place for a warm matcha tea after a long walk

Nightlife

The bars and clubs in Paris are amazing - but expensive!
Be careful with your money and do not be too intoxicated as it may attract thieves at the RER.

Transport

Use the RER to go around Paris!
It's very easy and cheap (the ticket machine is in English)
Here are the 3 airports and instructions on how to get to the center of Paris:

  1. Charles de Gaulle: Take RER B to Gare du Nord (North Station)
  2. Orly: Take RER C
  3. Le Bourget: Take the bus 350 towards Gare de l'Est (East Station)
