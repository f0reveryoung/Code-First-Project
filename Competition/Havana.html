Solo Female Traveller Tips

  • Not many people know this but Havana is one of the most safe places in the world
  • The exchange rate from Euro, USD and GDP are great so you don't have to spend excessively. You will be able to get
  • Havana is filled with tourists, so finding other solo travellers and young people is super easy
  • There will be many local people trying to sell you different items so be vigilant
  • You may find yourself waiting outside a drugstore, so be sure to carry sanitary products, hand sanitiser and deodrant
  • Mobile usage is regulated in Cuba, so Pay-as-you-go cards will be available in hotels and local shops
  • Water CANNOT be accessed from any tap, bottled water is your safest option

Food & Drinks
